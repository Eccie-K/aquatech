Welcome To Aquatech Water Technology

Aquatech Water Technology is a dynamic Kenyan company
with complete in-house facilities for the design, fabrication,
corrosion protection, transportation and erection of a comprehensive
range of municipal and industrial water equipment.
The company is registered as a 8ME contractor (reference number 107503),
with the Construction Industry Development Board and currently has a Level
2 B-BBEE score and a CSD registration #MAAA 0303945.
Our range of equipment manufactured and supplied is enormous
and the quality of our products legendary.




Why Aquatechnologies

  • Only manufacturer in Kenya that can manufacture all the equipment related to
    sewage treatment equipment.
  • Only Kenyan manufacturer of Archimedean screw pumps with an active
    reference list of more than 30 years.
  • Our first belt press sludge dewatering machines were fabricated during 1993 -
    currently agents for the Giotto ( previously known as Sernagiotto / Siemens ) belt
    presses and have access to supply other well know European equipment as well.
